Extra

Although we've already implemented plenty of convience and safety features, we do have some extra features that should hopefully make your life in our houses more enjoyable overall by adding some nice little things.

Everyone hates having to clean off dirty plates when they're in the sink, especially when they have food on them. To combat this we added a robotic arm that will scrape the food off the plates. This food will then fall into a garbage disposal and the dishes will automatically be placed into a dishwasher. Now you don't have to worry about touching dirty dishes!

Having to constantly check calendars, schedules, watches, phones, clocks, anything can get pretty inconvenient at times. However, we've added small speakers to each room that can be voice activated to announce the current time, date, and other information regarding potential events or schedules. Now, you can stay up to date in any room by simply asking. Also, the speakers will announce the time at the start of every hour except at night.
