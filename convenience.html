Convenience

The main purpose of our smart houses is to make your life easier, and that is what we have accomplished by adding a slew of features and robots that will help automate some of the boring housekeeping tasks. Some of the most important and useful utilities our smart house offers are, cleaning mice, automatic stove for cooking, automatic dishwasher, cigar dispenser, automatic car, automatic garage, clocks that announce the time, and many more.

Everyone hates having to clean off dirty plates when they're in the sink, especially when they have food on them. To combat this we added a robotic arm that will scrape the food off the plates. This food will then fall into a garbage disposal and the dishes will automatically be placed into a dishwasher. Now you don't have to worry about touching dirty dishes!

Having to constantly check calenders, schedules, watches, phones, clocks, anything can get pretty inconvient at times. However, we've added small speakers to each room that can be voice activated to announce the current time, date, and other information regarding potential events or schedules. Now, you can stay up to date in any room by simply asking. Also, the speakers will announce the time and any upcoming events at the start of every hour except at night. This will be handled by the brain which can detect when all the house occupants are in their beds, and will then mute the announcements until the voice alarms go off. You can also use these as alarm clocks. These voice clocks can be set to go off at a specific time by using an assortment of different sounds or introductions. Now, you have simply eliminated the need for an alarm clock at your bedside. You can also choose to snooze the clocks via voice commands. It is more cost effective to purchase these clocks because they make it easier for you to manage your time in general.
